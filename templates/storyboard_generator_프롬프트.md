# 스토리보드 생성 프롬프트 (시네마틱 광고용)

> 용도: 한 장의 에디토리얼 **스토리보드 시트**(A3 가로, 그리드)를 생성 → 그 프레임들로 10초 영상 제작.
> 재사용법: 아래 프롬프트에서 **CORE CONCEPT(주인공·여정·톤)**, **컬러팔레트**, **재질**, **6프레임 내용**, **브랜드 톤**만 내 것으로 바꾸면 됨. 6개 섹션 구조(골격)는 그대로 유지.
> 출력처: 이미지 생성 모델(GPT Image / Nano Banana / FLUX 등) 또는 Higgsfield Cinema Studio.

---

Create a high-end cinematic advertising storyboard sheet for a premium surreal fantasy commercial.
The output must be a clean editorial layout (A3 landscape, white background, grid-based design) combining: character references
environment design
color palette
material/style notes
camera storyboard sequence

---
CORE CONCEPT
A surreal dream-journey cinematic ad following a single female protagonist who transitions through layered dream realities: sleep → flying dream → dragon flight → mountain landing → skateboard descent → aerial flight → river surfing transformation.
The tone is cinematic, ethereal, emotional, and premium editorial with seamless surreal transitions between physical rules.

---
LAYOUT STRUCTURE (VERY IMPORTANT)
HEADER
Title: "ONE DREAM. FULL JOURNEY. FULL AD."
Subtext: "Cinematic surreal transformation storyboard breakdown"

---
SECTION 1: CHARACTER + HERO REFERENCE
One elegant female protagonist (high-fashion, minimal aesthetic, slightly dreamlike realism)
Provide 4–6 pose variations:
sleeping peacefully (eyes closed, soft light)
waking mid-air realization (confused, weightless expression)
flying posture (arms slightly open, drifting)
dragon riding pose (dynamic, gripping reins or mane, wind-swept)
skateboard descent stance (balanced, energetic motion)
surfing stance on water (flowing, stable, focused expression)
Clean studio lighting, neutral tones, soft cinematic skin rendering
Add "hero character reference sheet" look (casting board aesthetic)

---
SECTION 2: DREAM TRANSFORMATION ELEMENTS
Breakdown of surreal sequence elements:
Dream sleep state (soft bed, floating dust particles, dim light)
Aerial sky environment (cloud ocean, infinite sky depth)
Dragon companion (majestic, semi-realistic, mist-covered scales, glowing breath accents)
Mountain peak landing (windy cliff, dramatic scale, cinematic fog layers)
Skateboard manifestation (appears mid-air, glowing subtle energy edges)
River transformation (board transitions into surfboard seamlessly)
Water environment (glass-like reflective river, slow-motion ripples)
Macro cinematic texture notes: wind particles, mist density, motion blur trails, gravity distortion effects

---

A seamless morphing dream-world environment:
Layer 1: bedroom dissolving into sky
Layer 2: open aerial cloud dimension
Layer 3: dragon flight through volumetric fog valleys
Layer 4: mountain ridge cinematic landing zone
Layer 5: floating hill slope with surreal skateboard physics
Layer 6: river horizon merging sky and water boundary
Include:
floating terrain transitions
gravity-breaking movement paths
top-down motion diagram of travel route (sleep → sky → dragon → mountain → hill → river)
Lighting: soft volumetric god rays, cinematic dusk-blue palette, glowing atmospheric edges

---

Frame 1: Girl sleeping in bed, soft glow, dream particles forming above her head (macro close-up)
Frame 2: She wakes up mid-air, realizing she is flying in open sky (wide aerial shot)

Camera notes per frame:
macro / wide establishing / crane / tracking / slow-motion / aerial drone / low-angle hero framing
